Game Rules

The Goal: Avoid being the last player holding cards. That person is the "Budala" (Fool).

Attack & Defense

  • Attack: Play a card face-up. To attack again, match the rank of a card on the table.
  • Defend: Play a higher card of the same suit OR any Trump card.
  • Pass: Play a card of the same rank as the attack to "pass" it to your neighbor.

End of Round

  • Success: If the defender beats all cards, they are discarded. Defender attacks next.
  • Failure: If the defender cannot beat the cards, they pick up the pile and lose their turn.

Game Origin

Budallas is inspired by the classic game Durak, but with unique Balkan characteristics. The rules and game mechanics were influenced by Panther Hall 6th Floor.

It is a tradition that a player who loses 3 games in a row does a challenge given by the other players.
