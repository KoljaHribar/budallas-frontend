Game Rules

The Goal: Avoid being the last player holding cards. That person is the "Budala" (Fool).

Attack & Defense

  • Attack: Play a card face-up. To attack again, you must match the rank of a card already on the table.
  • Defend: You must play a higher card of the same suit OR any Trump card.
  • Pass: If you have a card of the same rank as the attack, you can play it to "pass" the attack to your neighbor.

End of Round

  • Success: If the defender beats all cards, they are discarded. The defender becomes the next attacker.
  • Failure: If the defender cannot beat the cards, they must pick up the whole pile and lose their turn to attack.

How to Play

Budallas is a game of attack and defense. There are no winners, only one loser, the "Budala".

Game Origin

Budallas is inspired by the classic game Durak, but with unique Balkan characteristics. The rules and game mechanics were influenced by Panther Hall 6th Floor.

It is a tradition that a player who loses 3 games in a row does a challenge given by the other players.
